# Randonneuring.org web application

Randonneuring.org provides several randonneuring event management tools intended to
be used by the RBA, Event Organizer, or webmaster of a region. 
When you enter your events and routes into randonneuring.org, you get a complete automated system for managing your 
brevets.  Get up to speed quickly with these tools. 
Read the RBA/Organizer [Quick Start Guide](https://randonneuring.org/about/quick_start).

The tools here can do several big things for your region.

### Main Functions of Randonneuring.org

The _Event Manager_ system at randonneuring.org captures the details of all your events. A _Roster Manager_
keeps track of who's riding (and validates their RUSA membership).
Then the CueWizard _Route Processor_ will take your route described in a tool like RWGPS (with some rando-specific markup added),
combine that with details of your event, and produce a variety of outputs. 

The automatically generated outputs include

- High Quality Cue Sheets (landscape and portrait)
- Brevet Cards in several formats
- Waivers with event details and rider names pre-printed
- Web Pages and JSON that you can incorporate in your website
- CSV results for RUSA upload

### eBrevet Support 
When you put your events into randonneuring.org, you automatically support the [eBrevet](https://github.com/ctnadovich/ebrevet#ebrevet) mobile phone app for rides, including
a Control Check-In status web page for your use. Although randonneuring.org is not required for using eBrevet, most regions will find it to be the easiest way to do so.

Want to know what to tell riders about eBrevet? Send them to the 
[eBrevet FAQ](https://randonneuring.org/about/ebrevet_faq).
